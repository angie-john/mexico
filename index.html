Welcome to Mexico!

Visa

-Visa is not necessary for stays under 180 days
-For longer trips a tourist visa is required

Major Cities

-Cancun
-Mexico City
-Tijuana

Language

-Spanish is widely spoken
-Some English is spoken in tourist areas -It's good to learn key phrases in Spanish

Culture

-Tipping culture is very important, similar to the U.S.
-Be sure to tip workers well
